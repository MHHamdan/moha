About Me

Dr. Mohammed Hamdan is a cutting-edge researcher in Machine Learning (ML), Large Language Models (LLMs), and Computer Vision (CV). With a Ph.D. from École de Technologie Supérieure (ÉTS), Montreal, Canada, and extensive experience in integrating Generative AI, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), and vision-language systems, he specializes in developing state-of-the-art AI solutions.

As a pioneer in LLM engineering, Mohammed leverages models like GPT, RAG, and QLoRA, combined with tools like HuggingFace, LangChain, and Gradio, to tackle real-world challenges in healthcare, document analysis, and customer support automation.
